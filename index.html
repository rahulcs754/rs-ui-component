Developed Beautiful Websites Quickly with A Modern Responsive front-end.
RS(rise and shine) is a CSS library, integrated with pre-defined styled classes, and Utilites, for a quick creation of websites, with much focus on Functionality. Just copy below link and put into css file.
@import url("rs-ui.netlify.app/css/style.css")
OR
Copy the Link given below and paste it in the tag of your html, above all other style sheets.
<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://rs-ui.netlify.app/css/style.css">
